Our Story

The Storyteller of Wine

The story of Pérenne is the story of Rohit Prinja Sharma, a small-town boy from Batala, India, who chased the American Dream to the rolling hills of Healdsburg, California. Inspired by his mother, Prem, Rohit's journey is one of passion, dedication, and a deep-seated desire to create something eternal.

Guided by the mentorship of Kerry & Daisy Dameski and the encouragement of his friend David, Rohit founded Pérenne—a name meaning "eternal"—as a tribute to the enduring strength and grace of women and mothers everywhere. It's more than wine; it's a legacy rooted in love and respect.

Our Sonoma County Vineyards & Origins

Our roots run deep in Healdsburg, the heart of Sonoma County. This historic town, with its charming plaza and world-renowned terroir, is where our journey began. It started not in a sprawling vineyard, but in a local institution: Healdsburg Liquor & Market, the origin spot where a passion for wine took hold.

We are inspired by the rich storytelling of wine country and are committed to crafting wines that speak of this unique place. As we grow, we carry a vision of expanding our vineyards, even dreaming of bringing the Pérenne spirit to the landscapes of India, blending our heritage with our future.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard and expedited shipping options. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping arrives within 2-3 business days. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size. We currently ship to most states within the US.

What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not, we will replace or refund your order. Please contact us at hello@pperenne.com within 30 days of receiving your order to initiate a return. Please note that we can only accept returns of unopened bottles.

How does the Wine Club membership work?

Our Wine Club offers two tiers: Gold and Platinum. As a member, you'll receive quarterly shipments of our wines, exclusive access to limited releases, discounts on all purchases, and invitations to special events. You can manage your membership, including shipments and preferences, through your online account.

Can I customize my Wine Club shipment?

Yes, members have the flexibility to customize their quarterly shipments. You can log in to your account before each shipment to select the wines you'd like to receive from our available collection. Customization is a key benefit of being part of the Pérenne family!
