Our Story

The Storyteller of Wine

The story of Pérenne is the story of Rohit Prinja Sharma, a small-town boy from Batala, India, who chased the American Dream to the rolling hills of Healdsburg, California. Inspired by his mother, Prem, Rohit's journey is one of passion, dedication, and a deep-seated desire to create something eternal.

Guided by the mentorship of Kerry & Daisy Dameski and the encouragement of his friend David, Rohit founded Pérenne—a name meaning "eternal"—as a tribute to the enduring strength and grace of women and mothers everywhere. It's more than wine; it's a legacy rooted in love and respect.

Our Sonoma County Vineyards & Origins

Our roots run deep in Healdsburg, the heart of Sonoma County. This historic town, with its charming plaza and world-renowned terroir, is where our journey began. It started not in a sprawling vineyard, but in a local institution: Healdsburg Liquor & Market, the origin spot where a passion for wine took hold.

We are inspired by the rich storytelling of wine country and are committed to crafting wines that speak of this unique place. As we grow, we carry a vision of expanding our vineyards, even dreaming of bringing the Pérenne spirit to the landscapes of India, blending our heritage with our future.
