Our Story

From Sonoma, with Love

Pérenne is more than wine; it is an exquisite experience born from the beautiful Sonoma County terroir. It is the perfect pour for honoring strength, celebrating life's moments, and savoring romance.

Our journey began with a deep respect for the land and a passion for crafting wines that reflect the unique character of Sonoma County. We believe in sustainable farming and gentle winemaking techniques to create wines of elegance and distinction.
